# Privacy Policy for DVSA Test Booker Chrome Extension

_Last updated: 2025-11-30_

## Introduction

DVSA Test Booker is a Chrome extension designed to automate the booking of DVSA practical driving tests. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Data Collection

The extension may collect and store the following information locally on your device:
- Driver licence number
- Test reference number
- Search postcode
- Preferred test dates and locations
- Extension configuration and settings

**No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.**  
All data is stored using Chrome’s extension storage and is only accessible to the extension itself.

## Data Usage

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of automating and managing your driving test bookings.  
We do **not** use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose unrelated to booking automation.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.  
Your data remains private and is only used within the extension.

## User Control

You can view, update, or delete your stored information at any time using the extension’s settings.  
Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data from your browser.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.  
However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here and, where appropriate, notified to you via the extension.
